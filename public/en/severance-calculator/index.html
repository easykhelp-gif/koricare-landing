Korea Severance Pay Calculator

Worked 1+ year in Korea? You're entitled to severance pay (퇴직금) regardless of nationality or visa status. Enter your salary and length of service.

What is severance pay (퇴직금)? Money your employer must pay if you worked at one workplace for 1+ year. Roughly “one month's average wage × years worked.” Foreign and undocumented (overstayed) workers are all included.

💼 If you hold a valid visa (E-9·H-2) Most of your severance is already saved in “Departure Guarantee Insurance (출국만기보험).” But if your legal severance is more than the insurance payout, the employer must pay the difference. Don't just take the insurance and leave — check for a remaining gap.
🛡️ Even if undocumented / overstayed Your right to severance still stands (Korean Supreme Court precedent). Without departure insurance, you must claim directly from the employer, and you can appoint a representative to claim even after returning home — within 3 years. Even if the boss threatens to “report you to immigration,” since Nov 2025 labor offices do not report wage-theft victims to immigration.
